## 01. Logo

### Brand Architecture

GeniusPet is a **master brand**. Sub-brands are product-focused extensions within one coherent system.

**GeniusPet defines:**
- A visual system
- The tone of communication
- A basic color palette

**Sub-brands add:**
- Product specialization
- Their own accent color
- A focused message

### Naming Logic

All sub-brands use a unified structure: **GENIUS + product**

| Common Name | Product |
|-------------|---------|
| GeniusTag | ID Tags |
| GeniusVet | Vet Telehealth |
| GeniusInsurance | Insurance |
| GeniusMedication | Medication |

**Logo Components:**
- Logogram (icon/symbol)
- Logotype (text)

---

### Logo Safespace and Size

#### Safespace
Always maintain clear space around the logo to ensure legibility and visual impact.

**Clear Space Rule:**
The minimum clear space equals the height of the Logogram. No text, graphics, or other elements may enter this area.

#### Minimum Size
To preserve readability and brand integrity, do not scale the logo below the minimum sizes specified.

| Format | Minimum Height | Recommended |
|--------|----------------|-------------|
| Digital | 20px | 48px |
| Print | 5mm | 13mm |

---

### Usage on Backgrounds

When using our logo, it is important to consider the background on which it will be placed to ensure optimal visibility and legibility.

| Background Type | Logomark | Logotype |
|-----------------|----------|----------|
| Light Backgrounds | Red (#D74242) | Dark (#2B2B2B) |
| Dark Backgrounds | Red (#D74242) | White (#FFFFFF) |
| Colored Backgrounds | Use dark or white logo depending on contrast |

---

### Invalid Logo Usage

Incorrect usage of the logo can dilute the impact of the brand and create confusion among audience.

**DON'T:**
- Apply gradients, shadows, or effects
- Change the position or size of elements
- Move, replace, or delete elements
- Use low-contrast backgrounds
- Recreate using different fonts
- Change colors
- Rotate, skew, or shear
- Use complex/busy backgrounds

## 03. Typography

### Primary Typeface

**Gilroy** is the core typeface of the GeniusPet brand. It combines modern geometry with warmth and clarity, supporting both a friendly tone and a professional presence.

#### Headlines
**Gilroy Semibold** is used for all headings and key statements. It delivers confidence, structure, and strong brand recognition.

#### Emphasis in Headlines
**Gilroy Light Italic** is used to highlight individual words or phrases within headlines. This creates contrast, guides attention, and adds a distinctive brand voice without breaking consistency.

#### Body Text
**Gilroy Regular** is used for all paragraphs and longer text. It ensures optimal readability, clarity, and a clean, approachable tone across all communications.

## 04. Imagery & Illustration

### Photography

GeniusPet photography should feel **warm, real, and emotionally engaging**. Images must be natural, well-lit, and authentic—never staged or overly commercial. We show genuine moments between pets and their owners, focusing on care, trust, and everyday life.

**Key Principles:**
- Natural lighting
- Authentic moments
- Emotional connection
- Care and trust themes
- Everyday life scenarios

---

### Illustrations

Illustrations are **minimal, modern, and purpose-driven**. They support content, explain ideas, and enhance clarity without replacing real photography. Style should remain clean, simple, and friendly, using brand colors and avoiding unnecessary detail or decorative complexity.

**Style Guidelines:**
- Balanced between flat and dimensional (not overly cartoonish, yet warm, approachable, and contemporary)
- Solid color fills with subtle shading to add depth
- No gradients, glow, or visual effects
- No outer contour stroke—only internal lines to define details and structure

**Style Spectrum:**
- ❌ Too flat
- ✅ Brand style (balanced)
- ❌ Too dimensional

---
